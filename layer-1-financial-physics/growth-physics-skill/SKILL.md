## üéØ Core Concepts

### Growth Physics Framework

Every service business has ONE primary constraint that limits growth:

1. **DEMAND** - Not enough leads or customer interest
   - Symptoms: High close rate, low lead volume
   - Solution: Improve marketing and lead generation
   - Example: AC Styles (lead magnet optimization)

2. **DELIVERY** - Limited capacity to serve customers
   - Symptoms: High demand, team at capacity
   - Solution: Systematize and scale team
   - Example: Amy Lash and Beauty (technician scaling)

3. **EFFICIENCY** - Unit economics don't work
   - Symptoms: Margins declining as you scale
   - Solution: Reduce costs or improve pricing
   - Example: Garvey Disposal (route optimization)

4. **QUALITY** - Consistency issues limit retention
   - Symptoms: Good sales, high churn
   - Solution: Build systems and standards
   - Example: Multi-location operations

5. **CAPITAL** - Needs investment to scale
   - Symptoms: Can't grow without significant capex
   - Solution: Secure funding or adjust model
   - Example: Waste management (truck fleet)

6. **RETENTION** - High churn limits LTV
   - Symptoms: Good acquisition, but customers leave
   - Solution: Improve product/service quality
   - Example: Professional services (client retention)

7. **PRICING** - Commoditization pressure
   - Symptoms: Price competition, race to bottom
   - Solution: Differentiate or target segments
   - Example: Waste management vs. HOA specialization

### Key Formulas

**Unit Economics Foundation**
```
LTV:CAC Ratio = Customer Lifetime Value / Customer Acquisition Cost
Healthy Ratio: ‚â• 3:1 (target 5:1+)
```

**Churn Impact on LTV**
```
True LTV = Monthly Profit / Monthly Churn Rate
5% reduction in churn = 2x increase in LTV
```

**Revenue Per Customer**
```
LTV = (Gross Margin Per Customer) / (Churn Rate)
= (Revenue Per Customer - Cost Per Customer) / Churn Rate
```

**Capacity-Based Scaling**
```
Revenue = Customers √ó Transaction Frequency √ó Average Ticket Value
Revenue = Capacity Utilization √ó Throughput √ó Price
```

## üìö Real-World Service Business Examples

### Featured Case Studies

The skill includes 8 detailed case studies covering different service verticals, constraints, and scaling patterns:

#### 1. Garvey Disposal - Waste Management
- **Revenue:** $642K | **Margin:** -23%
- **Constraint:** Efficiency (route optimization needed)
- **Key Metrics:** CAC $67 | LTV $1,300 | LTV:CAC 19.4:1
- **Challenge:** Negative margins despite strong LTV:CAC ratio
- **Solution:** Route efficiency and asset utilization
- **Playbook:** Garbage Collection (referenced in High Ticket Services)

#### 2. AC Styles - Personal Styling
- **Revenue:** $309K | **Margin:** 42%
- **Constraint:** Demand (lead generation)
- **Key Metrics:** CAC $600 | LTV $10K | LTV:CAC 16.7:1 | Churn 2.5%
- **Challenge:** Lead volume too low despite healthy economics
- **Solution:** Exceptional lead magnet and demand generation
- **Playbook:** `playbooks/personal-styling-services.md`
- **Key Insight:** When unit economics are great, scale demand, don't fix supply

#### 3. Kyo Chiropractic Chain - Health Services
- **Revenue:** $5.2M | **Margin:** 23% | **Scale:** 6 locations
- **Constraint:** Demand (lead flow insufficient)
- **Key Metrics:** CAC $700 | LTV $3,400 | LTV:CAC 4.9:1 | Close Rate 71%
- **Challenge:** High close rate suggests room for pricing power
- **Solution:** Scale high-intent channels (Google Ads, Local SEO)
- **Playbook:** Health Fitness playbook
- **Key Insight:** 71% close rate is 2x healthy average (30-40%) - raise prices

#### 4. Trophy Outlet - Hybrid Retail + Service
- **Revenue:** $1.72M | **Margin:** 6.4%
- **Constraint:** Efficiency (channel mix)
- **Challenge:** Retail component pulls down margins
- **Solution:** Emphasize high-margin services, B2B corporate programs
- **Key Insight:** Identify high-margin components and expand them

#### 5. Unfiltered Hospitality - Beverage Consulting
- **Revenue:** $1.1M | **Margin:** 11.3%
- **Constraint:** Delivery (consulting leverage)
- **Key Challenge:** Project-based model limits revenue
- **Solution:** Productize audits, build retainer offerings
- **Playbook:** `playbooks/bar-beverage-consulting.md`
- **Key Insight:** Separate high-leverage work (audits) from implementation

#### 6. Legal Services for Musicians - Professional Services
- **Revenue:** $300K | **Margin:** -33%
- **Constraint:** Delivery (broken unit economics)
- **Key Challenge:** Hourly billing + high service cost = unprofitable
- **Solution:** Value-based packages ‚Üí retainers ‚Üí productized services
- **Playbook:** `playbooks/professional-services.md`
- **Key Insight:** Fix unit economics before scaling; don't grow broken models

#### 7. Amy Lash and Beauty - Beauty Services
- **Revenue:** $2.3M | **Margin:** 28% | **Scale:** Multi-location chain
- **Constraint:** Delivery (technician capacity and quality)
- **Key Challenge:** Scaling requires finding and retaining skilled technicians
- **Solution:** Systematize training, build quality standards, multi-unit playbook
- **Playbook:** `playbooks/beauty-services.md`
- **Key Insight:** Labor-intensive services scale through systems, not just hiring

#### 8. Basil & Co Thai Restaurant - Food Service
- **Revenue:** $3.5M | **Margin:** 19%
- **Constraint:** Efficiency (channel mix and operations)
- **Key Challenge:** Thin margins typical; need smart channel strategy
- **Solution:** Shift revenue mix to high-margin channels (catering, corporate)
- **Playbook:** `playbooks/restaurant-hospitality.md`
- **Key Insight:** Don't grow low-margin channels; develop high-margin ones
